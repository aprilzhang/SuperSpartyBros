I Wanner Super Sparty Bros.

Created by April Zhang (Adapted from the original Super Sparty Bros)

  • Play Online
    (WebGL browser support required，try firefox)
  • Download Windows x86 Version

Game Description:

Collect coins, avoid enemies and traps, and go for the rose!


Game Modifications:

  1. Modification 1: Completed level 1 and created level 2.
  2. Modification 2: New obstacles: waterfall and spike weed. (Trick: press space for a longer time to jump higher)
  3. Modification 3: New enemy: An underground moving spike weed!
  4. Modification 4: New music in level 2. https://www.freesound.org/people/Setuniman/sounds/369718/
